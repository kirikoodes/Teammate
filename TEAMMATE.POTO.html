How it works

Listens to the microphone continuously and records sounds into a 48-slot corpus. Once 4 slots are filled, it starts improvising — responding to what it hears with its own phrases.

POtO — real-time granular synthesis on the last 4 seconds of audio. Grain size and spread on p.7.

8OS — 20s looper. K3 → REC records. K3 → TRANS plays back as random grains.

MGEN — 16-channel MIDI sequencer. Enable channels on p.14, pick styles, start on p.13. Breaks (p.15) apply to active channels for one cycle. Evo (K2 on p.14) sets a per-step mutation rate — notes drift to adjacent scale degrees, gates and velocities shift gradually each cycle.

MIDI routing — each stream (IMPRO / POtO / 8OS) can be routed to up to 4 MIDI devices on pages 9–12.

Requires Chrome/Edge + mic permission. MIDI requires HTTPS or localhost.
